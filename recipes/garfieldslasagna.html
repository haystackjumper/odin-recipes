Garfield's Lasagna

Dish Description

This is Garfield's favorite dish! A good meaty lasagna, nice and red with delicious melty cheese.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ketchup
  • string cheese

Instructions

  1. Line casserole dish with lasagna noodles.
  2. Squirt ketchup on top of noodles.
  3. Separate string cheese into single layer
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 until lasagna reaches desired height.
  5. Put oven to 350 degrees.
  6. Bake lasagna for 30 minutes.
